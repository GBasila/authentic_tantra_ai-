What's inside

Four weeks together

Eight people. Not a course. Not a webinar. Not a marathon. A small circle, where daily practice unfolds.

Each week — one live call, 90 minutes. I lead the practice, we are silent together, we share if we wish, we answer questions. It is not a lecture — it is a circle.

Each day — a short voice message from me in a private Telegram group. Two to three minutes. One thought, one practice, one invitation a day.

Once across the four weeks — a personal 15-minute voice exchange with me alone. Not a consultation. A quiet, personal «hello» midway through.

Not therapy. Not coaching. Not technique.
A return — at your own pace, alongside others.

Four weeks

What unfolds across 28 days

Week 1 · Coming home.
Basic practices of return to the body. A hand on the heart. The breath that was always there. A first meeting with what you haven't heard for a long time.

Week 2 · Listening.
Micro-attention to the body throughout the day. Where does it contract? Where does it soften? The body learns again to be a voice.

Week 3 · Presence.
Carrying the practice into living life. The kitchen. A conversation. A queue. A cup of tea. Kaula is not a temple ritual. It is presence where you already are.

Week 4 · What remains.
A daily practice that fits inside a real life. What you take with you after the Circle — as a rhythm, not as a task.

For whom

This is for you, if

  • you have been living «in your head» for a long time, and the body has grown quiet
  • you want to begin a practice — but you are not ready to walk it solo
  • it matters to you to be in an intimate group, not a large audience
  • you are ready to give 15–20 minutes a day to practice for four weeks
  • you want to see and be seen — but not to «expose» yourself

This is not for you, if

  • you are looking for a fast «result» in four weeks
  • you want «techniques» or certification
  • you are in an acute situation that needs psychotherapeutic care
  • group dynamics are not possible for you right now

In both cases — I thank you for honesty with yourself.
If 1:1 feels closer — come to a session.

Often asked

A few answers in advance

When does the cohort start?

The first cohort starts within two to three weeks after applications close. The exact date is sent in reply to your application so you can put all four calls in your calendar.

Live calls usually take place on Saturday morning Moscow time — this works for participants from Europe, Russia, Asia. Final time is agreed with the cohort after enrollment.

What if I can't make a live call?

Every call is recorded. The recording arrives in the private group within 24 hours. Still, please come live when you can — the circle works differently in real time.

If you miss two or more calls — I will suggest moving you to the next cohort. This is not «punishment»; the practice is simply such that live presence gives a different experience.

Is this only for women?

No. The Circle is open to everyone. The first cohort will most likely be women aged 32–48 — that is the strongest pull I see. If you are a man and it resonates — come.

What language are the meetings in?

Calls and daily voice messages take place in your language.

Each cohort runs in one language. If English-speaking participants apply — I gather a separate cohort for them with its own circle, its own calls, and its own voice messages in English.

I do not mix languages in one circle — the depth of practice asks that everyone hear each other and me without translation.

The personal 15-minute exchange — in whatever language is comfortable for you.

I've never practiced before. Is this for me?

Yes. The Circle is built to fit both those just opening to practice and those who have practiced for a long time. I explain all the basics from zero, and depth comes from within, not from experience.

I've already done a lot. Won't this be too basic?

No. What we do is not «new techniques». It is a return to what you already know but do not always live.

Most experienced practitioners who come to circles like this say: «I remembered something I had long known but lost».

What if I want to leave midway?

This rarely happens, but it is possible. You can leave — I do not hold anyone. A refund is not offered in this case — because the seat is already taken, and your presence or absence on calls does not change the cohort's costs.

If you need to leave due to force majeure — write to me personally, we will find a way.

And the 1:1 session — is that different?

Yes. The 1:1 session is 75 minutes with me alone, €200, a deeper and more targeted work. The Circle is four weeks alongside others, $ 100, an intimate group and daily practice.

Some begin with the Circle and then come to a 1:1. Some do the opposite. Both paths are right.
