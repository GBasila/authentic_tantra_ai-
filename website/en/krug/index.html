What's inside

Eight weeks together

12–15 women. Not a course. Not a webinar. Not a marathon. A small circle where slow, precise work unfolds with what is already in you.

Each week — one live call, 90 minutes on Zoom. I lead the practice, we are silent together, we share if we wish, we answer questions. It is not a lecture — it is a circle.

Each day — a 15–20 minute audio practice. At your own pace, in your own time. Access to recordings stays with you forever.

A private Telegram group — for questions between calls and shared movement. Not a stage, a quiet room.

Bonus: one 30-minute private session with me, anytime during the course. A quiet, personal «hello» midway through.

Not therapy. Not coaching. Not «technique».
Depth — at your own pace, alongside others.

Eight weeks

What unfolds across 56 days

Week 1 · Return.
First touch with the body as home. The core practice — Authentic Breath. Silence as foundation.

Week 2 · Listening.
Micro-attention to the body throughout the day. Distinguishing the voice of fear from the voice of knowing.

Week 3 · Boundary.
Body as temple. Working with «yes» and «no» — not from the mind, but from deep bodily truth.

Week 4 · Feeling.
Emotions as energy, not problem. Working with frozen feelings through breath and sound.

Week 5 · Intimacy with self.
Solo Kaula practices — what then transfers naturally into relationships. Not «how to be better with a partner», but «how to be deeper with yourself».

Week 6 · Voice.
Voice as channel of the body. Sound, breath in sound, spontaneous expression. Reclaiming the right to be heard.

Week 7 · Devotion.
Entering the heart. Practice as living prayer — without words, through the body. Connection with what is larger than you.

Week 8 · Integration.
Gathering what you've passed through into a daily rhythm. Your own personal rule of practice for the months ahead.

For whom

This is for you, if

  • you have already built an outer life — career, home, relationships — and feel something critical is missing inside
  • you have done dozens of courses, retreats, therapies and no longer believe «another technique» will change anything
  • it matters to you to be in an intimate group of 12–15 women, not a large audience
  • you are ready to give 3–4 hours a week to practice for eight weeks
  • you want to walk into the tradition — rooted, grounded, without esoteric performance

This is not for you, if

  • you are looking for sexual techniques or «activation of feminine energy»
  • you want fast transformations in 21 days
  • you need a guru who will tell you what to do
  • you are in an acute situation that needs psychotherapeutic care

In both cases — I thank you for honesty with yourself.
If 1:1 feels closer — come to a session.

Often asked

A few answers in advance

When does the cohort start?

The first cohort starts within two to three weeks after applications close. The exact date is sent in reply to your application so you can put all eight calls in your calendar.

Live calls take place on Tuesdays at 19:00 Moscow time (17:00 in Berlin, 23:00 in Bangkok, 11:00 in New York). Final time is agreed with the cohort after enrollment.

What if I can't make a live call?

Every call is recorded. The recording arrives in the private group within 24 hours. Still, please come live when you can — the circle works differently in real time.

If you miss two or more calls — I will suggest moving you to the next cohort. This is not «punishment»; the practice is simply such that live presence gives a different experience.

Is this only for women?

Yes. «Authentic Depth» is a closed circle of 12–15 women. This is an intentional choice: in a female field a different depth arises, and the work with body, voice, and boundaries unfolds differently when only women are around you.

Separate formats for men and couples will be opened — follow announcements or write to me personally.

What language are the meetings in?

Calls and daily voice messages take place in your language.

Each cohort runs in one language. If English-speaking participants apply — I gather a separate cohort for them with its own circle, its own calls, and its own voice messages in English.

I do not mix languages in one circle — the depth of practice asks that everyone hear each other and me without translation.

The personal 15-minute exchange — in whatever language is comfortable for you.

I've never practiced before. Is this for me?

Yes. The Circle is built to fit both those just opening to practice and those who have practiced for a long time. I explain all the basics from zero, and depth comes from within, not from experience.

I've already done a lot. Won't this be too basic?

No. What we do is not «new techniques». It is a return to what you already know but do not always live.

Most experienced practitioners who come to circles like this say: «I remembered something I had long known but lost».

What if I want to leave midway?

This rarely happens, but it is possible. You can leave — I do not hold anyone. A refund is not offered in this case — because the seat is already taken, and your presence or absence on calls does not change the cohort's costs.

If you need to leave due to force majeure — write to me personally, we will find a way.

And the 1:1 session — is that different?

Yes. The 1:1 session is 75 minutes with me alone, €200, more targeted one-time work. «Authentic Depth» is eight weeks in a circle of 12–15 women, €450, with a bonus 30-minute private session inside.

Some begin with a 1:1 and then enter the cohort. Some do the opposite. Both paths are right.
