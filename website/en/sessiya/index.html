The most direct way to begin — is to meet one to one.

Perhaps you are here because something has gone unheard for too long.

Perhaps the body has started speaking louder
than you are used to hearing it.
Perhaps you have reached the point where «everything is fine»
no longer works.

Either way — you are welcome.
I know this place.

What happens

One hour, fifteen minutes

We meet — online or in person in Moscow. No forms, no questionnaires. Just — come.

The first minutes — conversation. Not an «anamnesis», but a quiet looking at what you have brought. What wants to be heard today. You do not need to know in advance.

Then — the work. It may be breath. It may be a practice with the body. It may be silence. It may be a conversation, in which something long unsaid finally surfaces.

At the end — a quiet closing. Perhaps one question that stays with you for a week. Perhaps simply a feeling that you have returned.

I do not «deliver a result». I walk beside you, while you remember what you need.

Whom this is for

This is for you, if

  • you feel that you have been living «in your head» and the body has become distant
  • you are successful on the outside, and empty within
  • you have tried yoga, therapy, meditation — and something is still untouched
  • you want to begin something real, and are ready to move at your own pace
  • you feel a resonance with what I write — and you know the time has come

This is not for you, if

  • you are looking for a fast result
  • you want «techniques» to optimize yourself
  • you are in a crisis state that requires psychiatric or psychotherapeutic support
  • you expect one hour to resolve what took decades to build

In either case — thank you for the honesty with yourself.

Frequently asked

A few answers in advance

Is this about sex?

No. Sexual energy in the Kaula tradition is one of many forms of life energy, and we work with it very gently, only when it is organic to your work. In a first session, most often we do not touch this topic at all.

The work is about returning to a living body, breath, and heart. Not about technique. Not about provocation.

Do I need to know anything about tantra in advance?

No. No prior knowledge is needed. I do not «check» your level and do not give «homework» to prepare. Come as you are.

Does this work in the online format?

It does. I hold many online sessions with people around the world. Most of the work — through voice, breath, directed attention. It does not require physical contact.

That said, if you can be in Moscow for your first session — I recommend in person. The depth of a first meeting in living contact is different.

What if I cry, or something strong arises in the session?

That is normal. And often it is the most important thing that can happen. I hold the space. There will be no «come on, pull yourself together». Tears, silence, sensations in the body — whatever arises, all of it is welcome.

Are follow-up sessions needed?

Often — yes. One session is an opening of a door. The real work unfolds in the second, third, sixth meeting. I do not «sign you up» for a program — but at the end of the first session, we will look together at whether continuing makes sense.

Is this suitable for couples?

Couple sessions are a separate format, and the investment is different. If you would like to work as a couple, mention this in your application — I will share the format separately.

What if I'm not ready?

Readiness is not required. What is needed is only the willingness to come as you are. If fear holds you back — this is a normal sign that something living is beginning to move. The session is precisely about giving that movement a soft space.

If something within you has already said «yes» —
I will be waiting for your letter.
