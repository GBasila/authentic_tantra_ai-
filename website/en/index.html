My path

Victoria

For twelve years I built a business.
A life in which I could deny myself nothing.
A body I did not want to return to.

On the outside — everything one could dream of.
On the inside — a quiet question that would not let me sleep:

«Is this all?»

I tried everything that promised an answer.
Retreats across countries. Dark retreats.
Plant-medicine diets of strength in Peru.
Places of power — where the earth remembers more than we do.

Each step stripped away another layer.

Then — Kaula.
Not as technique. As a way to live.
Practice at five in the morning. Every day. For years now.
And a quiet recognition: Love is the deepest teacher.

Today my work is to be a guide.
Not to teach — but to walk you toward the place I myself now stand.

If what once stirred in me
is stirring in you now —
you are not alone.
The door is ajar.
