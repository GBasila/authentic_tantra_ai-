# Email Architecture

All emails are written as *letters*, not campaigns.
They open with a sensory moment from the last 48 hours.
Subject lines are plain, never clickbait.

## Cadence
- **Tuesday 7am MSK** — «Утренняя практика» (weekly letter)
- **Friday 5pm MSK** — «Пятничное приглашение» (week's invitation)
- **Ad-hoc** — launch sequences, retreat announcements

## Welcome Sequence (7 emails over 14 days)

| # | Day | Subject | Intent |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 0 | сегодня утром (addressed to you) | Welcome, first breath practice |
| 2 | 1 | почему я встаю в 5 утра | Founder story Act 1–2 |
| 3 | 3 | то, что тело помнит | Introduce body memory concept |
| 4 | 5 | одно простое возвращение | First micro-practice audio |
| 5 | 8 | что такое Каула | Tradition lineage |
| 6 | 11 | пригласить (a whisper) | Introduce flagship existence |
| 7 | 14 | если вы остаётесь | Community invitation |

## Weekly Letter Template
- **Length:** 400–600 words
- **Opening:** sensory moment from last 48h (morning practice, a conversation, silence)
- **Body:** one teaching or reflection
- **Close:** invitation to one practice or offer (soft)
- **Signature:** «С теплом, Виктория»

## Rules
- No emoji in subject lines.
- No exclamation marks anywhere.
- No "exclusive," "limited," "last chance."
- Never "Hi [first_name]!" — address by presence, not by name-merge.
- Every email includes one link, never three.
