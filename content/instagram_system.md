# Instagram System

## Cadence
- **5 feed posts/week** — 3 Reels + 2 carousels
- **Daily stories** — raw, voice-first, not scheduled
- **Highlights** — 5 evergreen: About · Practice · Retreats · Couples · Free

## Post Types

### 1. Teaching Reel
**Length:** 45–60s. **Opens:** Victoria in natural light, one breath before speaking.
**Structure:** hook → teaching → soft invitation.
**Hook examples:**
- «Вы не устали. Вы не в теле.»
- «Тантра — не то, что вы думаете. И не то, что вам продают.»

### 2. Story Reel
**Length:** 60–90s.
**Structure:** sensory moment → realization → invitation.
Drawn from founder story or student story (with consent).

### 3. Practice Reel
**Length:** 30–45s.
**Structure:** "Take a breath with me" → guided micro-practice → completion.

### 4. Carousel — Teaching
**Slides:** 7–10.
**Structure:** title / question → 5–7 slides unfolding one idea → final slide = invitation.

### 5. Carousel — Poetry
**Slides:** 5–7.
**Structure:** single sentence per slide, full-bleed typography on brand palette.

## Caption Structure
1. **Hook line** (one sentence, resonates with pain or desire)
2. **Three-act body** — situation → turning → opening
3. **Soft CTA** — invitation to comment, save, or click bio link
4. **Question** — one question for the reader

## Hashtag Policy
- Maximum 3 hashtags per post.
- Never trending tags for reach.
- Branded: #АутентичнаяТантра #ПамятьТела

## What never appears on IG
- Countdown stickers for launches
- "Link in bio" screaming
- Before/after body photos
- Trending audio used literally (reinterpret or silence)
