# Instagram System (v2)

## Cadence

- **10 feed posts/week** (vs 5 in v1):
  - 4 Teaching Reels (Lineage, Embodiment, Story, Practice, Provocation, Relationship)
  - 2 Lifestyle Reels (Pillar 6 — vignettes with low voiceover or silence)
  - 2 Carousels (1 Document + 1 Poetry)
  - 2 Lifestyle Photo Carousels (3–5 still frames)
- **Daily 4-story arc** — see §«Stories» below. Stories are non-negotiable.
- **Highlights** — 6 evergreen: About · Practice · Couples · Travel/Place · Samadhi-equivalent (if/when paid container exists) · Free Lead Magnet

## Post Types

### 1. Teaching Reel (Pillars 1, 2, 3, 4)

- **Length:** 30–60 sec
- **Opens:** one of the F1-F5 storytelling formulas (see `/content/storytelling_framework.md`)
- **Structure:** hook → teaching → soft invitation
- **First 3 sec rules:** lead with paradox or specific number · don't show full face in frame 1 · silence is acceptable hook
- **Hook examples:**
  - «Вы не устали. Вы не в теле.»
  - «По вере вашей. Не больше.»
  - «То, что вы называете рутиной — это пропущенный шаг.»

### 2. Lifestyle Reel ★ new in v2 (Pillar 6)

- **Length:** 20–35 sec
- **Opens:** vignette frame, no person on screen for first 2 sec
- **Structure:** 3–4 stitched 5-sec vignettes, low voiceover OR silence + ambient
- **Voiceover rule:** if used, one line maximum; spoken at low volume, never «explained»
- **Examples:** «Это моё утро уже семь лет.» · «Не церемония. Просто тот час, в который я возвращаюсь.» · `[silence]`

### 3. Story Reel (Pillar 3)

- **Length:** 30–60 sec
- **Structure:** sensory moment → realization → invitation
- **Source:** founder Acts 1–5 or anonymized student story (with written consent — see `/content/storytelling_framework.md` ethics)

### 4. Practice Reel (Pillar 2)

- **Length:** 30–60 sec
- **Structure:** «Сделайте со мной» → guided micro-practice (live, not «demonstration») → completion
- **Rule:** Victoria must actually do the practice on camera. Viewers feel the difference.

### 5. Carousel — Document (Pillars 1, 4, 5) ★ new in v2

- **Slides:** 10–15
- **Structure:** title + N×point + invitation
- **Typography:** dense Cormorant Garamond serif. One idea per slide, screenshot-worthy as a standalone.
- **Title naming:** «N + noun» — «Семь признаков...», «Пять состояний...», «Три различия...»
- **Save mechanism:** designed for «save and return» behavior. This is the highest save-rate format on RU premium-feminine IG.

### 6. Carousel — Poetry (Pillars 3, 6)

- **Slides:** 5–7
- **Structure:** single italic sentence per slide, full-bleed typography on brand palette
- **Use case:** emotional / poetic content where one phrase per slide creates rhythm

### 7. Lifestyle Photo Carousel ★ new in v2 (Pillar 6)

- **Slides:** 3–5
- **Source:** real photos from a single morning, walk, or moment
- **Editing:** subtle warm grade, no filter overlay, no text on image
- **Caption ≤ 12 words.** The images carry the meaning.

## Caption Ceiling (new in v2)

| Format | Max words |
|---|---|
| Reel | **12** |
| Lifestyle Reel | **8** |
| Carousel (poetry) | 25 |
| Carousel (document) | 40 |
| Lifestyle photo carousel | 12 |
| Story | n/a (use voice or text overlay) |

**Old v1 caption structure (4-part: hook + 3-act body + CTA + question) is DEPRECATED for Reels.** The teaching lives in the video. The caption is the bumper sticker.

**v2 caption structure (Reels):**
1. One short phrase or paradox (1–2 lines)
2. Optional one-word invitation in 🤍 (e.g. «Возвращайтесь 🤍»)
3. Optional CTA («↓ напишите одно слово» / «↑ ссылка в шапке»)

**v2 caption structure (Document Carousel):**
1. Hook line — what the carousel gives the reader
2. One sentence of context
3. Soft invitation to save or share
4. Optional question

## Stories — daily 4-arc ★ new in v2

**Stories are not optional.** They are the substrate of the parasocial bond and the daily presence layer.

| Slot | Time МСК | Type | Content |
|---|---|---|---|
| **1 · Morning** | 07:00 | Phone-frame still | Window · cup · hand · candle. Text overlay ≤ 5 words. |
| **2 · In practice** | 12:00 | Voice-note story (45 sec) | Face usually off-camera. One thought, observed mid-day. |
| **3 · Beauty seen** | 18:00 | Phone-frame still or 5-sec clip | What caught Victoria's eye today (a fruit on a plate, a corner of light, a hand on a book) |
| **4 · Question for tomorrow** | 21:00 | Text on cream background | One line ending in a question. Seeds tomorrow's morning. |

**Topics for the 4-arc:**
- ✅ Morning practice (windowsill, cup, hand on heart — no full face)
- ✅ BTS — preparation for retreat, journey to teacher, the road
- ✅ Poetic note of the day — one phrase on screenshot, cream background
- ✅ DM-question answer (only if asked without aggression)
- ✅ Book she's reading (the spine, a paragraph)
- ✅ A meal that was made slowly (no plating-as-flex)
- ✅ A garden, a balcony, a tree branch

**What NEVER appears in stories:**
- ❌ Polls/sliders/counters of marketing type
- ❌ «Свайп вверх — купи»
- ❌ Birthdays, dinners with friends, life events (this is a different brand)
- ❌ Sponsor placements
- ❌ Faces of other people without their explicit consent

## CTA Library (v2 — operational, never decorative)

**Soft response CTAs (Reels):**
- «↓ Какое слово первым пришло, пока смотрели?»
- «↓ Сохраните. Перечитайте утром.»
- «↓ Если узнали — одно слово в комментариях.»
- «↓ Оставьте 🤍, если откликается.»

**Lead Magnet CTA (used as the bottom-of-caption bumper on most Reels from June 15 forward):**
- «↑ Бесплатно: 5 дней возвращения в тело — ссылка в шапке.»

**1:1 sessions CTA (only on Pillar 1, 3 posts in soft-launch weeks):**
- «↑ Заявка на сеанс — в шапке.»

**DM-driven CTA (1 Reel/week max):**
- «↓ Напишите мне в DM слово „возвращение" — я пришлю практику.»

**What never appears as CTA:**
- ❌ «Подпишись», «лайкни», «поделись» (commanding tone — voice violation)
- ❌ «Свайп вверх», «жми в шапку» (urgency tone)
- ❌ Anything with exclamation marks
- ❌ Any countdown

## Success metrics (v2)

We do **not** optimize Instagram for reach. Premium brand metrics:

| Metric | Healthy signal |
|---|---|
| Save rate (Reels) | > 8% |
| Save rate (Document Carousel) | > 15% |
| Comment quality | long, emotional, in full sentences (not «🔥») |
| DM responses | the most important metric — who writes personally is the future client |
| Profile visits | growing month-over-month |
| Story completion rate | > 70% — signals stories are watched, not skipped |
| Followers growth | secondary — quality > quantity |

A post with 200 likes and zero emotional comments is not a success.
A post with 50 likes, 3 long comments, and 5 DM messages is a victory.
