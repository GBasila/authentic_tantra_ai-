## Hook construction principles

1. **One sentence, one breath.** If it can't be said in one breath, it's not a hook.
2. **Body before mind.** Hooks that land in the body precede ones that engage the mind.
3. **Specific over abstract.** "Я не помню, когда плакала" beats "I feel disconnected."
4. **Reframe, don't shout.** The hook turns a familiar feeling on its side.
5. **Never click-bait.** A hook that doesn't deliver kills trust.
