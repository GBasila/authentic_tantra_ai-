# Five Content Pillars

Every piece of published content belongs to one of these five pillars.
Weekly mix should hold the ratios below; quarterly audit ensures we stay balanced.

## Pillar 1 — Demystification (25%)
**Purpose:** What tantra actually is. Debunking neo-tantra + pop distortions.
**Example topics:**
- «Три мифа о тантре, которые мешают вам её понять»
- «Почему тантра — это не про секс»
- «Каула: традиция, о которой вы не слышали»

## Pillar 2 — Embodiment Practices (25%)
**Purpose:** Give tangible body-level tools. Proof of the practice.
**Example topics:**
- Guided 7-breath reset
- Three-minute body-scan for over-thinkers
- Morning practice: return to the body in 10 minutes

## Pillar 3 — Relationship & Intimacy Wisdom (15%)
**Purpose:** For couples and those in relationships.
**Example topics:**
- «Уязвимость как путь к близости»
- «Что мешает глубокой связи — даже когда вы любите друг друга»
- Polarity work: masculine and feminine as energies, not roles

## Pillar 4 — Transformation Stories (20%)
**Purpose:** Founder's journey + student voices (with consent).
**Example topics:**
- Acts 1–5 of Victoria's arc
- «Что изменилось за 6 месяцев» — integrated student story
- Before / during / after of a retreat participant

## Pillar 5 — Science Bridge (15%)
**Purpose:** Ground ancient wisdom in modern research. Builds trust with skeptics.
**Example topics:**
- Polyvagal theory × tantric breathwork
- Somatic research × body memory
- Neuroscience of presence

---

# Mix Ratios (weekly)

For ~7 pieces of cornerstone content per week:
- 2 Demystification
- 2 Embodiment
- 1 Relationship
- 1 Story
- 1 Science

## Pillar rotation rule
Never publish two pieces from the same pillar back-to-back.
Alternate depth and accessibility.
