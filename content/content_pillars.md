# Six Content Pillars

Every piece of published content belongs to one of these six pillars.
Weekly mix should hold the ratios below; monthly audit confirms balance.

> **Voice law (applies to all pillars):** whisper not shout · invite not instruct · body-language over concept · short sentences with long breaths between them.

---

## Pillar 1 — Lineage & Demystification (15%)

**Purpose:** What Kaula actually is. What tantra isn't. Establish the unfair advantage of lineage.

**Psychological function:** Removes noise. Earns the А3 (skeptic). Gives the А1 something true to repeat to her friends.

**Example topics:**
- «Что такое Каула — в семи различиях»
- «Почему тантра — это не про секс»
- «Настоящие учителя — почти всегда не хотят, чтобы их так называли»
- «Чем эта работа отличается от того, что продаётся под словом „тантра"»

**Typical formats:** Reel (provocation hook) · Document Carousel (10–15 slides, save-bait)

---

## Pillar 2 — Embodied Practice (20%)

**Purpose:** Tangible body-level tools. Proof that the work is felt, not just thought.

**Psychological function:** Gives the viewer their first taste. Once the body responds, the brain trusts.

**Example topics:**
- Three-minute return to body (hand on chest, hand on belly)
- Seven breaths for over-thinkers
- Morning return — ten minutes
- Eye-gazing as practice (for couples or solo)

**Typical formats:** Reel — guided micro-practice (Victoria practicing live, not «demonstrating»)

---

## Pillar 3 — Story / Founder & Witness (20%)

**Purpose:** Victoria's path + student stories (with consent). Identity-building through narrative.

**Psychological function:** Parasocial trust. The viewer doesn't just learn from Victoria — they begin to *know* her.

**Example topics:**
- Acts 1–5 of Victoria's arc (founder story)
- «Что изменилось за 6 месяцев» — student integration (anonymised)
- «Я помню утро, в которое впервые услышала тишину внутри»
- «Самый страшный момент в моей семейной жизни был самый тихий»

**Typical formats:** Reel (story 30–60s, episodic) · Long Carousel (5-act arc broken across slides)

---

## Pillar 4 — Relationship & Polarity (10%)

**Purpose:** For А2 couples and А1 in partnership. Polarity as energy, not gender roles.

**Psychological function:** Activates the second largest avatar (А2 — couples). Also gives А1 something to send to her husband.

**Example topics:**
- «Близость — это не техника. Это согласие быть увиденным.»
- «То, что мы называем рутиной — это пропущенный шаг.»
- Polarity work: masculine and feminine as energies inside one person
- «Семь признаков, что пара живёт как соседи»

**Typical formats:** Reel (story or teaching) · Couple-coded lifestyle stills (no faces — two cups, two pairs of slippers, hand-to-hand)

---

## Pillar 5 — Science Bridge (10%)

**Purpose:** Ground ancient practice in modern research. Build trust with the А3 skeptic.

**Psychological function:** Permission for the rational mind to relax. «Тантра — это конкретная работа с нервной системой».

**Example topics:**
- Polyvagal theory × tantric breathwork (Stephen Porges)
- Bessel van der Kolk × body memory
- Neuroscience of presence
- Esther Perel × Sue Johnson — what science says about couples losing intimacy

**Typical formats:** Document Carousel (academic, citation-rich) · occasional Reel with science quote on screen

---

## Pillar 6 — Living Practice / Lifestyle (25%) ★ new in v2

**Purpose:** Show what Kaula looks like in an actual life. Proof-of-practice content. The texture of conscious living.

**Psychological function:** ***This is the magnetism layer.***

The other five pillars teach what we believe. Pillar 6 lets viewers *see what it looks like to live it* — the morning, the cup, the candle, the walk, the doorway, the breath visible in the chest. The viewer doesn't say «I want to learn from her». They say «I want to live like that». That's identity transfer — the strongest psychological hook in the genre.

**Critical brand discipline.** Pillar 6 is NOT lifestyle-for-vanity. Every Pillar 6 post must include at least one of:
- A pause — the camera lingers on a breath, not an outfit
- A practice gesture — hand on heart, palms on the ground, breath visible
- A teaching whisper in voiceover — one line, not a speech
- A moment of presence — the candle flame held for 4 seconds, the steam rising for 6

**Example topics:**
- «Это моё утро уже семь лет» — vignettes of the actual morning
- «Не церемония. Просто тот час дня, в который я возвращаюсь.» — slow tea pour
- A walk through her apartment at dusk, candles lit one by one
- Hand brushing tall grass at sunset
- Two cups on a windowsill (couple-coded)
- A still life: книга на столе (Bessel van der Kolk visible), чашка, окно

**Typical formats:**
- Lifestyle Reel (vignette-style, 4 stitched 5-sec frames with low voiceover or silence)
- Lifestyle Photo Carousel (3–5 still frames from a single morning)
- Daily Stories (the 4-story arc — see `/content/instagram_system.md`)

**What Pillar 6 is NOT:**
- Bikini content (off-brand for Victoria — even though it works in the genre)
- Travel-as-luxury-flex (always show the practice inside the place, never the place alone)
- Outfit/skincare/cafe haul style
- Curated «day in my life» that competes with the teaching pillars for screen time

# Mix Ratios (weekly)

For ~10 cornerstone posts per week (new cadence — see `/content/instagram_system.md`):

| Pillar | Posts/week | Slot examples |
|---|---|---|
| 1 · Lineage & Demystification | 2 | Mon teaching Reel + Tue document Carousel |
| 2 · Embodied Practice | 2 | Fri morning practice Reel + Wed mini-practice |
| 3 · Story / Founder & Witness | 2 | Wed story Reel + Thu poetry Carousel |
| 4 · Relationship & Polarity | 1 | Rotates Mon or Fri |
| 5 · Science Bridge | 1 | Once per fortnight in Tue Carousel slot |
| 6 · Living Practice / Lifestyle | 2 | Mon evening Reel + Wed evening photo Carousel |

**= 10 feed posts/week** (vs current 5/week). Plus daily 4-story arc.

---

## Pillar rotation rules

1. **Never publish two posts from the same pillar back-to-back.** Alternate depth and accessibility.
2. **Every week opens with Pillar 1 or 3** (teaching or story) and closes with Pillar 6 or 2 (lifestyle or practice).
3. **A Pillar 6 post must run within 24 hours of any high-intensity Pillar 1 or 3 post.** Lifestyle softens the edges of teaching/story.
4. **Pillar 5 (Science) runs no more than 2× per month.** Over-use makes the brand feel academic, not embodied.
5. **Pillar 4 (Couples) runs in concentrated weeks** (e.g. a full week dedicated to А2) rather than scattered — pattern recognition builds for the А2 segment.

## Per-pillar caption ceiling (new in v2)

| Format | Max words in caption |
|---|---|
| Reel | 12 |
| Lifestyle Reel | 8 |
| Carousel (poetry) | 25 |
| Carousel (document) | 40 |
| Lifestyle photo carousel | 12 |
| Story | n/a (use voice or text overlay) |

**Rationale:** The teaching lives *in* the Reel or *across* the carousel slides. The caption is a wink, a hook, or a CTA — never a recap of what the viewer just saw. Long captions kill the algorithm by suppressing the «more» tap rate. See `/strategy/instagram_magnetism_upgrade.md` PART 1.3 for the data.

---

## Brand-voice non-negotiables (audit checklist before publishing)

Before posting from any pillar:

- [ ] No exclamation marks in brand copy
- [ ] No emoji except 🤍 or 🕊️ (and only in stories or rarely in Reel captions)
- [ ] No forbidden phrases (просветление за N · открой свою сексуальность · мастер тантры · etc.)
- [ ] No countdown timers, no fake scarcity, no «только сегодня»
- [ ] Russian first (no casual English mixing)
- [ ] One screenshotable line identified (which slide or which voiceover line is the takeaway)
- [ ] Caption within the per-pillar ceiling
- [ ] Avatar tagged (А1 / А2 / А3 / All)
- [ ] Pillar tagged
