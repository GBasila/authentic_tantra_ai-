## 4 · How to install in Telegram

### Step 1 · Replace the Channel Description (old text has typos and off-brand framing)

1. Open Telegram app (desktop or mobile)
2. Open the @AuthenticTantra channel
3. Tap the channel name at the top → opens channel info
4. Tap the pencil icon (Edit) — top-right on mobile, "Edit" on desktop
5. Find the **Description** field
6. Delete the old text:
   > "Transmiting spiritual methods and techniques which integrates ancient wisdom with modern understanding for awekening energy, awerness and ecstasy through bodywork, breathing, visualisation. Working with sexual energy we enter into expended consciousness."
7. Paste the new EN description from §1 above
8. Save

### Step 2 · Replace the profile picture

The current AI-art with chakras violates `/brand/visual_direction.md` (forbidden visuals: chakra diagrams, sacred geometry overlays).

**Best replacement options:**
- The same photo Victoria uses for @viktorynox Instagram — for cross-channel recognition
- The Monogram «AT» logo from `/strategy/instagram_magnetism_upgrade.md` PART 10 (will be exported as PNG when ready)

To change: Edit channel → tap profile picture → upload new image.

### Step 3 · Publish and pin the EN welcome message

1. In the channel, create a new post
2. Copy-paste the entire EN message from §2 above
3. Publish
4. Long-press (mobile) or right-click (desktop) on the message → "Pin"
5. Telegram will ask "Notify subscribers?" — choose NO (silent pin — brand-voice rule: invite, never shout)

### Step 4 · Publish and pin the RU welcome message

1. Create another new post
2. Copy-paste the entire RU message from §3 above
3. Publish
4. Pin it the same way (silent)
5. Telegram now shows both pinned messages at the top

### Step 5 · Reorder pins if needed

If you want EN to show first (since it's the primary language for this channel): pin EN second, RU first — Telegram shows the **most recently pinned message on top**. To put EN on top, pin RU first, then EN.

## 5 · Brand-voice audit notes

Both translations pass `/brand/tone_of_voice.md`:

**Forbidden phrases:** the words «тайное знание» and «техника» appear ONLY inside negative-denial framing ("Не тайное знание. Не техника, которую нужно выучить"). This is the brand's signature de-positioning move (separating from neo-tantra noise). Same pattern as `/content/instagram/2026-05/week-02-demystification.md` posts 6 and 10.

**Signature phrases preserved verbatim:**
- «Тут нечему учиться — здесь есть лишь то, что нужно вспомнить.» (the touchstone, per `/brand/brand_identity.md`)
- «Тело помнит то, что разум давно забыл.» (body-memory canon, per `/content/instagram/2026-05/week-03-body-memory.md` post 12)

**Voice rules honored:**
- "Мы её вспоминаем" — uses the «мы вспоминаем» pattern, not «я научу»
- "Проводник — не учитель, не гуру" — anti-guru positioning per founder context
- Short sentences with breath between them
- Single 🤍 at end only
- Zero exclamation marks (verified by grep before commit)
- Body-language vocabulary throughout: «земная», «поворачивается внутрь», «слышать», «возвращения в тело»

**Lifestyle/aspiration markers tastefully embedded:**
- "ретриты в Перу, тёмные ретриты, паломничества по Азии" — proof of lineage without name-dropping
- "двенадцать лет в крупном бизнесе" — credibility bridge for А3 skeptics (per `/audience/client_avatar.md`)
- "ежедневная практика с пяти утра" — quiet proof of discipline
