# The Seven Stages

Every person the brand touches moves through (or pauses at) one of seven stages.
Each stage has: an emotional truth, a content need, an offer, a ritual.

## Stage 1 — Stranger (Незнакомец)
**Emotional truth:** "Something is missing, but I don't know what."
**Discovers us via:** IG Reel, podcast mention, a friend.
**Content need:** Recognition — language for what they feel.
**Offer:** None yet. Just resonance.
**Ritual:** First save of a post.

## Stage 2 — Witness (Свидетель)
**Emotional truth:** "This feels different. I want to keep listening."
**Becomes witness by:** Follow on IG, podcast subscribe, email signup.
**Content need:** Consistent presence, depth, trust.
**Offer:** Free lead magnet, free masterclass.
**Ritual:** Completing the welcome sequence.

## Stage 3 — Student (Ученик)
**Emotional truth:** "I am ready to begin."
**Becomes student by:** First paid purchase.
**Content need:** Practical guidance, sense of path.
**Offer:** Tier 1 entry or Tier 2 flagship.
**Ritual:** Completing first module.

## Stage 4 — Practitioner (Практикующий)
**Emotional truth:** "The practice is becoming mine."
**Becomes practitioner by:** Completing flagship.
**Content need:** Sustained container, peer community.
**Offer:** Tier 3 membership.
**Ritual:** Showing up to weekly community practice 4+ weeks.

## Stage 5 — Devotee (Преданный)
**Emotional truth:** "I want to go deeper than the course can take me."
**Becomes devotee by:** First retreat or 1:1 container.
**Content need:** Intimacy with the teacher, transformational space.
**Offer:** Tier 4 premium.
**Ritual:** First retreat arrival.

## Stage 6 — Sister / Brother (Сестра / Брат)
**Emotional truth:** "This is my path. These are my people."
**Becomes by:** Multi-year continuity, active community leadership.
**Content need:** Seat at the table.
**Offer:** Long-term mentorship, co-facilitation opportunities.
**Ritual:** Returning to a second retreat.

## Stage 7 — Guide (Проводник)
**Emotional truth:** "I am called to carry this for others."
**Becomes by:** Certification track.
**Content need:** Lineage transmission, teaching skills, ethics.
**Offer:** Tier 5 certification.
**Ritual:** Lineage acceptance ceremony at completion.

---

# Progression Logic
- Never rush a stage. The system is designed to let people stay where they are.
- Not every witness becomes student. That is fine.
- Not every practitioner becomes devotee. That is fine.
- The ratio that matters: % of each stage that feels held, not % that ascends.
