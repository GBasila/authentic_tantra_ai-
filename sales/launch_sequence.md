# Canonical Launch Framework

Every flagship launch follows this arc. Specific launch copy lives in
`/sales/launches/YYYY-QN-offer/sequence.md`.

## Week -8 to -6 · Seeding (Посев)
**Content:** Pillar-heavy. No mention of launch.
**Emails:** Standard weekly letter rhythm.
**Audience work:** Heavy listening — surface emerging pains.
**Goal:** Build readiness without naming the door.

## Week -5 to -4 · Warm-up (Прогрев)
**Content:** Begin planting concept ("there is a practice that..."). No dates yet.
**Emails:** Begin weaving future reference ("when the time comes...").
**Goal:** Reader starts to sense something is approaching.

## Week -3 · Waitlist Opens (Список ожидания)
**Email 1 (Letter from Victoria):** "I am opening the doors again."
**Content:** First mention of flagship by name. Soft waitlist CTA.
**Goal:** Capture 500-1000 waitlist subscribers.

## Week -2 · Free Training Series (3 videos)
**Release cadence:** Video 1 Monday, Video 2 Wednesday, Video 3 Friday.
**Content:** Teaching only — no pitch.
**Emails:** Daily light touches pointing to each video.
**Goal:** Deepen trust + preview the practice.

## Week -1 · Anticipation (Ожидание)
**Email sequence:** Daily short letters, story-driven.
**Content:** Founder story cluster + student story.
**Goal:** Emotional ripening.

## Week 0 · Cart Open (Врата открыты)
**Day 1:** Opening letter. One clear invitation. No bonuses, no frills.
**Day 2:** Teaching letter — depth.
**Day 3:** Q&A letter — answer real questions from DMs/waitlist.
**Day 4:** Silence day (one single IG post; no email).
**Day 5:** Story letter — a student's journey.
**Day 6:** Soft reminder — "doors close on Sunday."
**Day 7:** Close letter — "The door is closing at sunset."

## Week +1 · Integration (Интеграция)
**No new offers. No "oh by the way."**
Welcome cohort with warmth. Silence the marketing.
Deliver the first week of the course with excellence.

## Week +2 to +4 · Retrospective
**Retrospective in:** `/sales/launches/YYYY-QN-offer/retrospective.md`
**Questions:**
- What did we offer that landed?
- What language did the buyers use back to us?
- What were the non-buyers feeling?
- What would we design differently next round?

# Guardrails for every launch
- No bonus stacks. The offer is the offer.
- No fake scarcity. If seats are capped, they're capped for a reason (capacity, not theater).
- No urgency timers on pages.
- The close is an actual close — no "extended by popular demand."
- Ads (if any) stop 48h before cart close. The final days are earned, not bought.
