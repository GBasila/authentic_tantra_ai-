# The Ascension Ladder

Every offer corresponds to a client-journey stage. Prices are premium by design —
they signal the depth of the work and attract committed students.

## Tier 0 — Free Entry (Stranger → Witness)
- **Lead magnets** — «5 дней возвращения в тело» (email challenge); body-scan audio (10 min)
- **Free masterclass** — «Тантра без мистики» (90 min, quarterly)
- **YouTube + Podcast** — always free
- **Purpose:** first embodied experience, email list growth

## Tier 1 — Entry (Witness → Student)
- **«Дыхание возвращения»** — 7-day paid mini-course · **€30-€80** · self-paced
- **Paid masterclass replay library** · **€50-€120** per session
- **Purpose:** first transaction; threshold of commitment

## Tier 2 — Flagship (Student → Practitioner)
- **«Аутентичное Пробуждение»** — 8-12 week flagship course
  - Format: video modules + weekly live calls + community + homework
  - Price: **€600–€900**
  - 2 live cohorts/year + evergreen between

## Tier 3 — Membership (recurring)
- **«Круг возвращения»** — private community
  - Weekly live practices, monthly Q&A, resource library, graduate leaders
  - Price: **€30–€50/month**
  - Target: 300+ members by end Year 1

## Tier 4 — Premium (Devotee → Sister/Brother)
- **Live retreats** — 2–4/year · **€3,000–€8,000** · 15–25 seats
- **1:1 Mentorship** — 6-month container · **€6,000–€12,000** · 6–8 seats/year
- **Individual sessions** — capped 6–8/month · **€300–€500/session**

## Tier 5 — Institution (Year 2+)
- **«Сертификация мастеров Аутентичной Тантры»** — 12–18 months
  - Price: **€8,000–€15,000**
  - Target: 8–15 graduates/year
  - Application-based only

# Pricing Principles
- 30-40% above nearest RU-speaking comparable.
- Always include scholarship tier (10% of seats).
- Never discount for urgency. Pricing reflects value, not market pressure.
- High-ticket (€3k+) is application-based, never one-click checkout.

# What each offer replaces in their life
- **Tier 1** — one evening of scrolling
- **Tier 2** — one month of therapy they've been putting off
- **Tier 3** — one gym membership they don't use
- **Tier 4** — one luxury vacation they've already taken three of
- **Tier 5** — one graduate degree that would not have changed who they are
