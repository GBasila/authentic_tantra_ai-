# Sequenced Team Build

Hire devotees before professionals. Every team member must be a practitioner
of the work, not only a service provider.

## Phase 1 — Foundation (Month 0-3) · 3 hires

### Operations Manager — FT · CRITICAL
- **Owns:** calendar, finances, vendors, project mgmt, launch logistics
- **Protects:** Victoria's practice time (5am sacred; no meetings before 11am)
- **KPI:** Victoria >70% time in practice/teaching/creating; zero missed commitments
- **Start:** Month 0

### Content Manager / Producer — FT · CRITICAL
- **Owns:** captures Victoria 2x/week, edits, schedules, archives
- **KPI:** 21 content pieces/week across channels; 100% publishing consistency
- **Start:** Month 1

### Copywriter (Russian, spiritual voice) — Freelance retainer · HIGH
- **Owns:** captions, emails, sales pages, retreat brochures
- **KPI:** open rate >45%; sales-page conversion >3%; zero voice complaints from Victoria
- **Start:** Month 1 freelance, full-time at Month 6

## Phase 2 — Amplification (Month 3-6) · 3 hires

### Creative Director — Freelance retainer · HIGH
- **Owns:** visual identity governance, art direction, design of major assets
- **KPI:** brand-consistency score; design review on every major asset
- **Start:** Month 3

### Community Manager — FT · HIGH
- **Owns:** Telegram community, weekly practice calls, member experience
- **KPI:** monthly retention >85%; weekly active rate >60%; NPS >70
- **Start:** Month 4

### Email/Funnel Specialist — Freelance · HIGH
- **Owns:** welcome sequences, nurture, launch sequences, evergreen funnel
- **KPI:** list growth 15% MoM; sequence-to-sale conversion >5%
- **Start:** Month 5

## Phase 3 — Scale (Month 6-12) · 4 hires

### Videographer / DP — Freelance monthly · MEDIUM
- **Owns:** cinematic YouTube, retreat documentation, course filming
- **KPI:** video completion rate >60%; YT 1k→10k subs in 9 months
- **Start:** Month 6

### Social Media Strategist — Freelance · MEDIUM
- **Owns:** strategy (not execution) across IG/TG/YT, trends, hooks, analytics
- **KPI:** reach 2x MoM for 6 months; save rate >8%
- **Start:** Month 7

### Paid Ads Manager — Freelance performance-based · MEDIUM
- **Owns:** Meta + YouTube ads for lead magnets and flagship
- **KPI:** ROAS >3x cold; CPL <$4 warm
- **Start:** Month 8 (no ads before Month 6)

### Executive Assistant — FT · MEDIUM
- **Owns:** inbox triage, scheduling, travel, personal logistics
- **KPI:** inbox-zero daily; zero scheduling errors
- **Start:** Month 10 (when Victoria crosses €500k ARR)

## Phase 4 — Institution (Year 2) · 3 hires

- **PR / Media Lead** — Freelance retainer
- **Brand Strategist (fractional)** — Advisor, quarterly
- **Certification Program Director** — FT when cert launches

# Hiring Principles
- Every hire completes a retreat within 6 months of joining.
- Founder interview is final stage for every hire.
- No second tantra teacher for at least 18 months.
- Specialists only. No generalist marketing managers.
- Outsource tech, in-house voice.
