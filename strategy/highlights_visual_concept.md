# PART 2 · The Cinematic Universe — How All 4 Highlights Connect

## 2.1 The visual rule that holds it all together

**Every photo passes through one of three lighting moods:**

| Mood | What it codes | Hex tint |
|---|---|---|
| **Dawn** (warm cream wash, soft amber) | Beginning, opening, invitation | warm |
| **Vignette** (dark warm, single source) | Intimacy, depth, sacred | dark warm |
| **Bright space** (cream + soft window) | Daily life, accessibility | light cream |

Each Highlight uses **all three** moods in its sequence — but **opens with a different one** to create visual rhythm in the profile.

## 2.2 The opening-mood map (left-to-right in profile)

```
[obo mne]    →   [1:1]      →   [dar]      →   [krug]
 DAWN open       BRIGHT       DAWN open      VIGNETTE
 (warm welcome)  (accessible) (gift / soft)  (sacred / deep)
```

This creates **emotional contrast** between adjacent highlights — viewer doesn't get tired of one mood.

## 2.3 The typography rule (unified across all)

- **Display:** Cormorant Garamond Italic, Light 300
- **Body:** Cormorant Garamond Regular, 400
- **Label/eyebrow:** Inter, Medium 500, all-caps, letter-spaced (only for «ДЛИТЕЛЬНОСТЬ» / «ФОРМАТ» type labels)
- **Color:** Charcoal `#2B2A28` on light, Cream `#F2EADF` on dark, Brass `#A68A5B` for accents only

**Never:** sans-serif для поэтических slides. Decorative fonts. Mixed weights.

## 2.4 The text-overlay rule (when photo is the background)

- **80% of frame = photo. 20% = breathable text area.**
- Text always in lower-third OR centered with gradient overlay for contrast
- Text gets **soft 30-40% black gradient strip behind it** if photo is busy
- Font size LARGER than slides without photos (photos compete for attention)

## 2.5 The cover-icon rule

Все 4 cover icons следуют **одной визуальной формуле** — но с разной charge'ой:

```
[Each cover]
  └── Cream background ИЛИ dark warm background
  └── ONE central element: photo OR single word OR symbol
  └── Brass accent (tiny dot or thin line)
```

3 из 4 covers — text-based (минимально). 1 — фото-based (Circle, как наш premium-anchor).

Это даёт **визуальную семью** при сохранении distinct-личности каждого.

# PART 4 · Overall Visual Identity Recommendations

## 4.1 The «cinematic universe» principle

All 4 highlights should pass this test:

> *If I screenshot the 4 covers side by side, do they look like the work of ONE art director making a single brand?*

Current proposed system:
- 3 text-covers (about / 1:1 / дар) in cream + Cormorant italic
- 1 photo-cover (круг) in dark warm + altar imagery

**This intentional asymmetry** = «круг» is the most premium/special. It's the *anchor* — eye is drawn to it.

## 4.2 Visual sequencing in profile

When all are uploaded, viewer sees (left to right):
```
[обо мне] [1:1] [дар] [круг] [Тай] [Перу]...
   ↓        ↓     ↓     ↓
  cream    cream  cream  photo
  text     text   text   dim warm
```

The **photo cover** of круг breaks the cream-text rhythm — eye snags on it, creates curiosity. **Strategic asymmetry.**

## 4.3 Story-to-story mood arc per highlight

Each highlight begins **bright** and ends **warm intimate**. Inside it should flow like a small film:

```
opening shot (warm welcome)
→ information section (cream + text)
→ depth moment (dim vignette)
→ closing CTA (warm glow + invitation)
```

This **mini-arc** keeps viewer engaged inside one highlight.

## 4.4 What ties them all together

| Element | Constant |
|---|---|
| Typography | Cormorant Garamond Italic + Regular Light |
| Color palette | Cream `#F2EADF` · Charcoal `#2B2A28` · Brass `#A68A5B` · Warm Dawn `#F5D9B6` |
| Light direction | Single warm source, never flat |
| Eye contact | Mostly down or away (no smile, no posed) |
| Body language | Slow, embodied (hand on chest, hand on cup) |
| Word for closing | «🤍» symbol — appears at end of every CTA |

This = **immediate brand recognition**.

## 4.5 What MUST NOT happen

- ❌ Mixing serif fonts (no «Playfair», no «Editorial New» — only Cormorant)
- ❌ Adding decorative elements (no swirls, no calligraphic flourishes)
- ❌ Photo with bright pink/red/purple dominating
- ❌ Speaking-event shots, microphone, audience-facing poses
- ❌ Hindu deities / Shiva statues primary frame
- ❌ Smiling-to-camera shots
- ❌ Heavy color grading (Hollywood orange-teal)
- ❌ Stock photos / fake-looking AI

## 5.2 Why this asymmetric system works

3 text + 1 photo = **rhythmic visual interest**. If all 4 were text → too monotone. If all 4 were photos → chaotic. The mix says: «we have intention».

## 5.3 Spacing rule for covers

Instagram crops covers to **150px circle on display**. Anything in outer 30% of the 1080×1920 image is **cropped out**.

Critical: **all text, symbols, faces must be in CENTER 60% of the image**.

Current text-covers I produced follow this rule. The photo cover for круг needs to be cropped tightly — central focus on altar/candle, no edge details.
