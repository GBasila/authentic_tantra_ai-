# Horizon

## Year 1 (2026) — Foundation
Theme: **«Возвращение»**
- Establish category ("Conscious Embodiment for Accomplished Souls")
- First flagship cohort (×2 live + evergreen)
- First live retreat
- Private community launched
- Team of 6-8 people
- **Revenue target:** €400-700k

## Year 2 (2027) — Institution
Theme: **«Круг»**
- Certification program launches
- Second language: English cohort (limited)
- Book proposal + traditional publisher
- Second live retreat per year
- Team of 12-15 people
- **Revenue target:** €1-2M

## Year 3 (2028) — Lineage
Theme: **«Передача»**
- Certified practitioners co-teach flagship
- International retreats (Bali, Portugal, Morocco)
- Book published
- Victoria reduces direct-fulfillment; increases authorship + curation
- **Revenue target:** €2-4M

---

# Growth Levers (in priority order)

1. **Category depth before reach.** Be the most sought name in a small category, not a middling name in a large one.
2. **Founder-led content** until M12. No ghost-written posts.
3. **Premium retreats** as brand amplifier — each retreat produces a year of content.
4. **Membership as compounding cash flow** — the most resilient revenue stream.
5. **Certification as institution-forming** — only launched after flagship is at v3+ maturity.

# Growth Principles
- **Say no to opportunities that don't fit.** We will refuse collaborations, media, and partnerships that dilute positioning. List of "yeses" kept short.
- **Do not scale the teacher. Scale the teaching.** Victoria's direct time stays sacred.
- **Pricing is a statement.** Never discount to grow volume.
- **Geography follows language.** English-speaking expansion only when we can hold the voice in English without thinning it.

# What we will not do
- Influencer partnerships outside our depth range
- Corporate wellness contracts
- "Tantric techniques" short-form courses
- Drop-ship anything
- Licensing the brand
