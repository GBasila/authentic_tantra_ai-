# 1 · CORE STRATEGY

## 1.1 The simplest business model

**Founder-led premium service business.** No SaaS, no membership site, no productized digital, no info-product launches in Month 1. Just two products at two price points, sold organically to an existing audience.

```
Free door  →  Lead Magnet «5 Days of Returning to the Body» (email-delivered, free)
Door 1     →  The Return Circle (4-week small group container) — €300 × 8 = €2,400
Door 2     →  1:1 Session (90 min, online or Phangan) — €400 × 5 = €2,000
```

That's it. Three layers, one of which is free. Everything else (mini-course, flagship, retreats) waits for Month 2-3.

## 1.2 Fastest monetization path — the order

1. **Day 1-7: Set the foundation.** Rebrand bios. Fix Telegram. Publish first lifestyle Reel on @viktorynox. No selling yet.
2. **Day 8-14: Sell 1:1 first.** Personal DMs + Reel announcement. 5 spots at €400. Cash starts flowing.
3. **Day 15-21: Build + tease The Return Circle.** Landing page, mechanics, registration. Tease via stories.
4. **Day 22-30: Open Circle registration + run a free masterclass.** Fill 8 spots. Total Month 1 revenue.

**Why this order:** 1:1 is operationally simplest, requires no new product build, uses existing /sessiya/ page. Generates cash and testimonials. The Circle (the actual NEW first product) gets time to build interest organically while 1:1 sales happen in parallel.

## 1.3 Easiest first offer to sell — explained

**1:1 sessions** are the easiest first offer to *SELL THIS WEEK* (everything exists; just need to open the door).

**The Return Circle** is the most important *PRODUCT to BUILD AND LAUNCH* in the next 30 days. It's the bridge that turns the brand from a one-woman-shop into a scalable practice.

The two are not competing — they fill different audience segments:
- 1:1 = those ready for deep solo work, want personal access to Victoria
- Circle = those want intimacy but not ready for €400 solo investment

## 1.4 Best audience positioning

**Tagline lock:** «Conscious embodiment for accomplished souls» (existing brand) — operationalized as **«For those who have already built everything outwardly and are now turning inward.»**

This positioning is already validated:
- In the IG bio (just locked)
- In the About text (just published in `/brand/about-victoria-en.md`)
- In every June post (already written)
- Speaks directly to А1 (women) + А3 (skeptics) avatars

**Do NOT broaden** to "anyone wanting tantra" or "anyone curious about spirituality." The narrow positioning is the moat.

## 1.5 Fastest trust-building strategy

Three layers of trust-building, **in parallel** during Month 1:

1. **Founder visibility** — Pillar 6 (Living Practice) lifestyle content on @viktorynox shows her *living* the practice. Not teaching it. Living it. The trust comes from witnessing texture.
2. **Bridge from @destiny.astro** — One bilingual bridge post mentions @viktorynox. The 25K audience (of which ~200-500 are active) is pre-qualified — already paid for spiritual guidance. The 5-10% who naturally migrate are warm.
3. **First buyer testimonials** — Once 1:1 sessions happen Day 14+, capture short anonymized voice-note testimonials with consent. Use sparingly in stories and as quote-cards.

## 1.6 Why this approach beats alternatives

| What we ARE doing | What we are NOT doing | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Selling 1:1 first (cash flow) | Launching a course in Week 1 | Course needs production, payment, sales page — too much for 30 days |
| Building Return Circle in parallel | Launching only 1:1 | 1:1-only caps at €2K/mo at founder time limit; Circle is scalable |
| Free LM as the soft door | Free webinar funnel | Webinar funnels need landing + email + automation we don't have yet |
| Personal DMs from founder | Mass cold outreach | Premium brand = handcrafted invitation, not automation |
| Use existing audience first | Spend on ads to grow | Existing audience is warm; cold is expensive and slow |
| One announcement per channel | Cross-post everything | Different audiences, different surfaces — see /content/instagram_system.md |
| Founder writes captions | Hire copywriter | Voice is the asset; cannot delegate |
| Two products only | Launch full offer ladder | Tier 2 (mini-course) and Tier 3 (flagship) wait for Month 2-3 |

## 1.7 Biggest mistakes to AVOID

1. **Don't launch the flagship course first.** Needs 80-120 students, polished landing, email automation. Premature.
2. **Don't use discount/countdown urgency.** Off-brand per `/brand/tone_of_voice.md`. Premium brands don't discount premium offers.
3. **Don't translate everything bilingual in one channel.** Each channel has one language. See `/content/telegram/ru-channel-launch.md`.
4. **Don't post the same content on @viktorynox and @destiny.astro.** Different audiences, different tones.
5. **Don't open more than 5 × 1:1 spots/month.** Victoria's practice time is sacred (5am daily). 5 × 2-hour sessions = 10 hours/month — already significant.
6. **Don't add team members.** Founder is the brand. Add operational support only after €10k/mo consistent.
7. **Don't build Kit / ConvertKit before having a free LM working.** Manual email send for first 20 LM subscribers is fine.
8. **Don't add Instagram ads in Month 1.** Organic only. Ads waste money before product-market fit is proven.
9. **Don't try to fix @destiny.astro in Month 1.** It's a sunset asset — one bridge post is enough.
10. **Don't fall into «I need a logo / website / brand book first» trap.** Everything functional exists. Bigger problem is *use it*.

# 2 · FIRST PRODUCT — «The Return Circle»

## 2.1 Naming

Working title: **«The Return Circle»** / **«Круг Возвращения»**

Why this name:
- «Return» — central brand verb (used in tagline, About, every post)
- «Circle» — implies group + ritual (a sacred shape)
- Bilingual symmetry (RU + EN both work)
- Distinctive but transparent (anyone understands what kind of thing this is)

Alternatives if she dislikes Return Circle:
- **«Living Practice»** — pillar 6 already named this
- **«Утренний Круг»** / **«Morning Circle»** — anchors to 5am ritual
- **«Первый Круг»** / **«The First Circle»** — emphasizes founding cohort
- **«Каула Круг»** / **«Kaula Circle»** — lineage-forward

**My pick: The Return Circle.** Most brand-aligned, most distinctive, works across both languages.

## 2.2 Format

A **4-week small group container.** Eight participants. Live + asynchronous mix.

**Weekly cadence (for participants):**

| Component | Frequency | Length | Format |
|---|---|---|---|
| Live group call with Victoria | 1×/week | 90 min | Zoom |
| Daily voice note from Victoria | 7×/week | 2-4 min | Telegram (private group) |
| Practice for the day | Daily | 5-10 min | Audio or text in TG |
| Private 1:1 voice note check-in | Once during the 4 weeks | 15-min audio exchange | Telegram |
| Optional question/reflection share | Anytime | Free | Telegram group |

**Total Victoria's time:**
- 4 × 90-min calls = 6 hours
- 28 × 3-min voice notes = ~90 min total
- 8 × 15-min 1:1 = 2 hours
- Group attention (reading shares, responding) = ~2-3 hours/week
- **Total: ~25-30 hours per cohort (over 4 weeks)**

vs 5 × 1:1 sessions (also ~10 hours direct + admin) — Circle is roughly 2.5-3× the time for the same revenue (€2,400 vs €2,000). **But:** Circle creates word-of-mouth, testimonials, community — that compounds.

## 2.3 Duration

**Exactly 4 weeks.** Not 6, not 8. Reasons:
- Short enough to commit to («I can do 4 weeks»)
- Long enough for real shifts
- Fits the Kaula «daily practice» framing (28 consecutive days of practice)
- Allows 2 cohorts/month if needed by Month 3

## 2.4 Transformation promised

**Headline promise** (for sales page, for the announcement post):
> *Four weeks. From living in your head to remembering your body.*

**Detail (what changes by Day 28):**
- You wake up and feel your body before your phone
- You notice when you leave the body during the day — and you can come back
- You have a daily practice (5-10 min) that fits into a real life
- You have a personal lexicon for what's happening inside you
- You're not «healed» — you're listening

**What we do NOT promise:**
- «Transformation in 28 days» (overpromise, forbidden by brand voice)
- «Heal your past» (we don't claim therapy outcomes)
- «Unlock your sexual energy» (off-brand entirely)

## 2.5 Price

**€300 per person.** Flat. No discounts.

Math:
- 8 spots × €300 = **€2,400 per cohort**
- Compared to Russian premium 4-week group containers: €200-500 range. €300 is solidly middle-premium.
- Compared to 5 × €400 1:1 sessions: same total revenue, more brand leverage.
- Compared to international Phangan retreats: a fraction. Reasonable for online container.

**Payment:** flat fee, paid in full at registration. No payment plans for first cohort.

**Currency:** EUR primary, accept USD/RUB equivalent if convenient for buyer.

## 2.6 Delivery model

Simplest possible technical setup:
- **Zoom** for weekly live calls (Victoria already has a Zoom account or uses free 40-min for short calls — for 90-min she needs Pro at €15/mo)
- **Private Telegram group** for voice notes, practices, community
- **Notion or single landing page** as the container's «home base» (optional in V1)
- **Email** for registration confirmation + practical onboarding

**No need for:**
- Course platform (Teachable, Kajabi, Thinkific) — premature, adds friction
- Mobile app
- Custom video player
- Forum software

**For payment:** bank transfer (international: PayPal, Wise, Revolut) — manual invoice for first cohort. Set up Stripe/CloudPayments for Cohort 2.

## 2.7 Onboarding flow

When someone registers:

1. **Day 0 (registration)** — they pay → automatic email: «Welcome. First call is [date]. Telegram group invite link below. See you there.»
2. **Day 0 evening** — they join Telegram group. Victoria posts a 2-min voice note: «Welcome. We start on [date]. Until then — nothing to prepare. Just notice when you feel your breath today.»
3. **Day 1** — first live call (90 min). Open with silence, introductions in 60 seconds each, one teaching, first practice together, close with the question for the week.
4. **Day 2-7** — daily voice notes + practices in Telegram. Group is alive.
5. **Day 8** — second live call. Same structure.
6. **Day 22** — fourth (final) live call. Integration. What stays.
7. **Day 29** — wrap-up voice note. Invitation to continue (1:1, future cohorts, retreats).

## 2.8 Positioning vs other offers

| Offer | What it serves | Price | Effort |
|---|---|---|---|
| LM «5 Days of Returning» | Curiosity, first taste | Free | Self-paced |
| **The Return Circle** | Wants intimacy + structure but not solo work | **€300** | **4 weeks group** |
| 1:1 Session | Wants deep personal access | €400/session | 90 min |
| (Future) Mini-course | Self-paced learners, lower budget | €60-90 | Self-paced |
| (Future) Flagship | Wants comprehensive program | €600-900 | 8 weeks |
| (Future) Retreat | Wants immersive in-person | €1,500-3,500 | 5-7 days |

The Return Circle sits between LM (free) and 1:1 (€400) — it's the **first paid step** for someone who wants real work but isn't ready for solo.

## 2.9 What's INSIDE the Circle — content arc

**Week 1: Coming home** — basic body return, hand-on-heart practices, breath
**Week 2: Listening** — micro-attention to body signals, what tightens/softens
**Week 3: Presence** — bringing practice into daily life (cooking, walking, conversation)
**Week 4: Continuation** — what stays after, daily ritual, the practice as a way of life

Each week has 1 voice teaching from Victoria + 5-7 daily micro-practices + 1 written reflection prompt.

# 3 · FASTEST WAY TO MAKE MONEY

## 3.1 What to sell FIRST (Day 1-14)

**1:1 sessions.** Open 5 spots. €400 each. €2,000 ceiling.

Why first:
- Infrastructure exists (sessiya page, application form, brand voice)
- Highest price per buyer (5 buyers = €2k)
- Operationally simplest (calendar + payment)
- Generates testimonials for The Return Circle launch

## 3.2 How to sell it

**Three sales channels, in priority order:**

### Channel A — Personal DMs (highest conversion, requires founder time)

Send personalized 80-100 word message to **10-15 close contacts** in Victoria's network:
- Bilingual depending on recipient
- Template in `/strategy/channel_acceleration_plan.md` §4
- NOT mass-blast. Each one personally adapted.

**Expected conversion:** 30-50% reply rate, 10-30% close rate. From 12 DMs → 3-6 closes.

### Channel B — Opening Reel on @viktorynox

Single Reel («Открываю пять сеансов в июне» — script in `/strategy/channel_acceleration_plan.md` §2 Variant A). Caption ≤ 12 words. Bumper: «Заявка — в DM или в шапке».

**Expected:** 50-200 views (small audience but warm), 5-15 DMs, 2-3 closes.

### Channel C — Telegram channel announcement

After RU channel is live (see `/content/telegram/ru-channel-launch.md`), publish one post in Week 2 announcing the 1:1 spots. Soft, not pushy.

**Expected:** 3-7 reactions, 1-2 closes.

**Combined Channel A + B + C = 5-11 closes from existing audience. Cap at 5 (Victoria's time).**

## 3.3 Sales process — the simplest version

```
DM / Reel response → 15-min discovery call (Zoom or voice) →
    if good fit → invoice sent → payment received → calendar booked → session happens
    if not fit → kind no, redirect to Lead Magnet or future Circle cohort
```

**No long sales pages. No webinars. No automation in Month 1.**

The 15-min discovery call serves two purposes:
- Victoria assesses fit (some people aren't ready for the depth)
- Buyer feels the energy match before committing €400

This is **brand-aligned and time-protective**. Both win.

## 3.4 Conversion assumptions (honest math)

**Audience reach in Month 1:**
- @viktorynox active followers: ~200-400 of 3K see any given post
- Personal network DM: 12 sent
- @destiny.astro bridge post: 200-500 see it actively
- TG RU channel (newly created): starts at 0, grows to 50-150 by Month 1 end

**Conversion funnel for 1:1 (target: 5 closes):**
- 12 personal DMs → 6 replies → 4 discovery calls → 3 closes (€1,200)
- 1 Reel on @viktorynox → 10 DM/inquiries → 5 discovery calls → 2 closes (€800)
- **Total 1:1: 5 closes × €400 = €2,000**

**Conversion funnel for The Return Circle (target: 8 closes):**
- Same audience but lower price point makes it easier
- Free masterclass in Week 4 → 30-50 attend → 6-10 register
- Soft CTA in TG channels + IG stories → 2-3 more register
- **Total Circle: 8 closes × €300 = €2,400**

**Month 1 total revenue target:**
- Floor: €3,600 (3 × 1:1 + 8 × Circle)
- Target: €4,400 (5 × 1:1 + 8 × Circle)
- Stretch: €5,600 (5 × 1:1 + 12 × Circle if waitlist opens early)

## 3.5 Best conversion mechanism — DMs > calls > posts > webinars

For premium brand 1:1 selling, ranked best to worst:

1. **DM personal invitation** — highest conversion, lowest reach
2. **15-min discovery call** — second highest, requires DM-to-call ask
3. **Soft post + DM-open** — broad reach, lower per-touch conversion
4. **Free masterclass / live event** — high reach + high conversion when product is ready
5. **Webinar funnel** — over-engineered for €400 product
6. **Sales page + cold ads** — wrong channel for premium first launch

For The Return Circle (group product), the order shifts:
1. **Free live masterclass** (Week 4) — best for converting cohorts
2. **DM to interested LM subscribers** — second
3. **Sales page + IG/TG announcement** — third

## 3.6 First revenue milestones (realistic)

| Day | Milestone | Revenue |
|---|---|---|
| Day 14 | First 1:1 paid | €400 |
| Day 21 | 3 × 1:1 paid | €1,200 |
| Day 25 | First Circle registration | €300 |
