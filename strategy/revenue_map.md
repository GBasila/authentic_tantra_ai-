# Cash Flow Anchors

- **Membership is the floor.** 300 members × €40 × 12 = €144k annual recurring by end Y1.
- **Flagship is the engine.** Bulk of cash Q3+Q1.
- **Retreats are the brand amplifier AND margin winner.** €3-8k × 20 seats × 30-50% margin.
- **1:1 is the halo.** Limited by Victoria's capacity; preserved for depth, not scale.

# Financial Principles
- 30% profit margin minimum on every offer. Below that, we've priced wrong.
- 20% of each launch → marketing fund for next launch.
- 10% of revenue → founder's practice fund (retreats, teachers, lineage travel).
- 6-month runway held in reserve at all times.
- Salaries paid first, founder's draw second.
