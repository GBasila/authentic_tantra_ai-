# About Victoria

A devoted practitioner of the Kaula tantric lineage.

Twelve years in executive business leadership before turning to the inner work. Daily practice from 5am. Retreats in Peru, dark retreats, pilgrimages across Asia.

A guide — not a teacher, not a guru. A fellow traveler with depth and lineage.

For those who have already built everything outwardly and are now turning inward — through one-on-one sessions, retreats, and circles. Not therapy. Not coaching. Not techniques. A return: to the body, to breath, to presence, to what has always been waiting.

The body remembers what the mind has forgotten.

> "Tantra calls me back, again and again, to the practice of serving love. The journey has shown me there is no greater gift than to surrender fully to love's presence. When I sit with others — in retreats, in circles, in the quiet of one-on-one — I open. Not as teacher. Not as healer. As a body through which love moves, without my agenda. Every encounter is an invitation to step into the unknown — trusting something larger than I understand."
