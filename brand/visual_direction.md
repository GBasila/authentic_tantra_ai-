# Visual Identity

## Reference Tier
Aspiration band: **Hermès × The School of Life × Heckfield Place × Aesop**.
Brand feels old, quiet, considered. Never trendy.

## Color Palette
| Name | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Terra Shakti | `#7A2E2E` | Primary accent — deep earth red, grounded passion |
| Cream Alba | `#F2EADF` | Primary background — warm cream, safety |
| Charcoal Silence | `#2B2A28` | Typography — grounded gravitas |
| Aged Brass | `#A68A5B` | Single metallic accent — maturity, not flash |
| Stone Breath | `#C8BFB1` | Secondary neutral for layered compositions |

**Never use:** bright white (#FFFFFF), pure black (#000000), saturated purples/pinks, neon.

## Typography
- **Headline:** A classical serif with wide letter-spacing (candidates: Canela, Söhne Breit, GT Sectra Display).
- **Body:** A humanist sans (candidates: Söhne, GT America Mono for accents, Inter).
- **Signature:** Handwritten script only for Victoria's name — never in body copy.

Rules:
- Generous letter-spacing in headlines (+20 to +40 tracking).
- Line-height 1.6–1.8 for body copy (room to breathe).
- Never bold-body-copy. Emphasis via italic or pull-quote only.

## Imagery Direction
- Natural light only. No studio flash.
- Subject matter: hands, breath visible, hair, shoulders, backs, nature textures.
- Bodies as sacred geometry — never sexualized, never performed.
- Film-grain preferred over digital sharpness. Medium-format feel.
- No stock photography, ever.

## What imagery never contains
- Chakra diagrams, kundalini serpents, lotus symbols
- Sanskrit overlays, mandala graphics
- Crystals arranged on bodies
- "Spiritual influencer" tropes (white flowing dresses in fields, etc.)
- Sexualized framing — any suggestion of neo-tantra aesthetics

## Motion / Video
- Shot length minimum 3 seconds. No hard cuts.
- Breathing-paced — cuts on exhale, never on energy.
- Ambient sound or single instrument (handpan, singing bowl, silence).
- Never upbeat music. Never trending audio.
- Color grade: warm, slightly desaturated, film emulation (Kodak Portra reference).

## Logo Principles
- Wordmark, not icon-mark.
- Generous whitespace around — minimum 1x cap-height of padding.
- Never on busy backgrounds.
- Never tilted, animated, or distorted.

## Retreat / Course Brochure Format
- Print-feel even in digital.
- Large poetic margins.
- One image per spread.
- Short body text. Long breath between paragraphs.
