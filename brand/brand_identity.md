# Brand Identity

## Name
**Аутентичная Тантра** (Authentic Tantra)

## Category
**Conscious Embodiment for Accomplished Souls** — a new category.
Not neo-tantra. Not esoteric mysticism. Not wellness.
The grounded, traditional path for people who already have outer success
and now seek inner depth.

## Archetype
- **Primary:** The Sage (60%) — wisdom, remembering, depth
- **Secondary:** The Lover (40%) — devotion, embodiment, sacred intimacy
- **Never:** The Magician (esoteric/mystical), The Outlaw (provocation)

## Founder
**Виктория** — devoted practitioner of the Kaula tantric tradition.
12 years in executive business leadership. Daily practice from 5am.
Retreats in Peru, dark retreats, plant-medicine paths, pilgrimage.
Not a guru. A fellow traveler with depth and lineage.

## Unique Selling Proposition
Traditional Kaula tantra, translated into a grounded daily practice
for people who already have everything except depth.

## Transformation Promise
> After six months, you will no longer need to search.
> Your body will remember.
> And from that remembering, everything in your life — work, love, choices — will re-organize itself.

## Meta-promise (one sentence)
**«Вернись к себе — туда, где тебя никогда не теряли.»**

## Signature Tagline
**«Тут нечему учиться — здесь есть лишь то, что нужно вспомнить.»**

## Client Journey Stages
1. **Stranger** — encounters brand for the first time
2. **Witness** — follows, reads, listens
3. **Student** — joins first paid offer
4. **Practitioner** — flagship course graduate
5. **Devotee** — membership + retreat
6. **Sister / Brother** — long-term community, 1:1 mentorship
7. **Guide** — certified teacher in lineage

## Emotional Territories We Own
- **Coming home** (возвращение домой)
- **Sacred safety** (священная безопасность)
- **Living prayer** (живая молитва)

## What We Are Not
- Not neo-tantra (sexual techniques as hook)
- Not esoteric show (chakras/kundalini as primary frame)
- Not therapy (we don't fix — we remember)
- Not coaching (no goal-setting, no productization of the soul)
- Not guru school (Victoria is проводник, not авторитет)
