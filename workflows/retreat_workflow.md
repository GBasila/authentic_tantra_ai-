# Retreat Lifecycle

## Phase 1 — Conception (6 months out)
- Victoria sets intention + theme
- Retreat Agent drafts brochure concept
- Ops sources location (2-3 candidates)
- Pricing decided, cohort size locked
- `/retreats/editions/YYYY-MM-location/` created

## Phase 2 — Announcement (4 months out)
- Brochure finalized
- Retreat page live
- Announcement to inner circle first (1 week head start for alumni)
- Then public waitlist
- Applications open

## Phase 3 — Fulfillment (4→1 month out)
- Applications reviewed weekly
- Acceptance letters sent (personal, signed)
- Pre-arrival sequence activated per accepted participant
- Logistics confirmed (transport, meals, supplies)
- Facilitation team briefing

## Phase 4 — Arrival (T-14 → T-0)
- Final pre-arrival emails sent
- Physical preparation of space
- Space blessing ceremony by facilitation team (T-1)
- Victoria arrives T-2 for her own integration time before holding

## Phase 5 — Retreat (T-0 to T-7)
- Daily flow per `/retreats/retreat_flow.md`
- Victoria's read overrides schedule
- Minimal outside communication (team handles all inquiries)
- Retreat is a closed container

## Phase 6 — Integration (T+1 → T+365)
- Integration sequence per `/retreats/integration_followup.md`
- Retrospective written within 2 weeks post-retreat
- Photos developed, printed, mailed to participants
- Handwritten cards sent

## Phase 7 — Archive & Learn
- All assets archived to `/retreats/editions/YYYY-MM-location/`
- Learning extracted → feeds `/retreats/retreat_structure.md` evolution
- Alumni added to long-term relationship map

---

## Approval gates
- Victoria approves all participant-facing materials
- Ops Manager owns logistics spine
- Retreat Director holds facilitator team coherence
- No changes to retreat arc less than 30 days out
