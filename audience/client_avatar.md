# Three Avatars

We serve three distinct people. Every offer, every post, every email
speaks to one of them specifically.

## Avatar 1 — Анна (Women Seeking Wholeness)
**Age:** 32–44
**Location:** Moscow, St Petersburg, Dubai, Cyprus, Berlin, London (RU-speaking diaspora)
**Profession:** senior manager, entrepreneur, consultant, or wife of successful husband
**Income:** €80k+ personal, or household €200k+
**Relationship:** married or long-term partnered, often with young children

### Inner landscape
- Successful by every external metric.
- Lives in her head. Disconnected from body's signals.
- "Over-functioning" — the family runs because she runs.
- Has tried yoga, meditation, therapy. Something still missing.
- Emotionally numb. Can't remember the last time she cried freely.
- Afraid that if she "feels fully," she might unravel her life.

### What she reads/watches
- The School of Life
- Russian-language depth podcasts
- Brené Brown (in translation)
- Esther Perel
- Instagram accounts that feel "quiet"

### What would move her
- Recognition without exposure
- Permission to soften
- A container that feels safe
- A teacher who is not performing spiritual purity

## Avatar 2 — Екатерина & Михаил (Couples Seeking Depth)
**Age:** 35–50
**Relationship stage:** 5–20 years together, often post-crisis or pre-crisis
**Children:** usually yes, 1–3
**Context:** love remains; connection has eroded

### Inner landscape
- Routine has replaced curiosity.
- Sexual connection has thinned or become perfunctory.
- They've tried couples therapy or are considering it.
- Want tools, not diagnostics.
- Wary of "spicing it up" content — they sense the problem is deeper.

### What would move them
- Framing that honors their maturity, not fixes them
- A container they can enter as a couple, not as patient
- Language of polarity + presence, not performance

## Avatar 3 — Алексей (Seekers of Aliveness)
**Age:** 30–48
**Gender:** often male (though 40% female in this segment)
**Life phase:** transition — career change, post-divorce, mid-life questioning
**Profession:** tech, finance, entrepreneurship, creative director

### Inner landscape
- "I have everything and I can't feel it."
- Intellectually curious, suspicious of "woo."
- Has read Sam Harris, Tim Ferriss, Jordan Peterson.
- Interested in plant medicine but won't say so.
- Wants authenticity badly; has scar tissue from previous spiritual bypasses.

### What would move him
- Science bridge content
- Victoria's business background as credibility
- Framing as "practice" rather than "spirituality"
- Depth without dogma

# Avatar Rules
- Every content piece notes which avatar it speaks to.
- No avatar is better than another. They need different doors.
- We do not create an avatar just because there's demand. We stay with these three.
