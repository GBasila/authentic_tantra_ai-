# The Before / During / After

For each avatar: where they begin, what happens in the work,
and what becomes possible on the other side.

## A1 — Women Seeking Wholeness

### Before
- Lives in head
- Can't feel body signals
- Emotionally flat
- Over-functioning, over-giving
- "I have everything, why do I feel nothing?"

### During (6 months of practice)
- Body awakens — sensations return
- Tears come back
- First real "no" without guilt
- Discovers she has desires again
- Begins listening to body's wisdom over mind's plan

### After
- Lives from the body, not above it
- Relationship with husband/children transformed — softer, present
- Chooses work from alignment, not ambition
- "I am no longer searching. I am here."

## A2 — Couples

### Before
- Love remains, connection has thinned
- Sex is perfunctory or absent
- Routine has replaced curiosity
- Private conversations with therapists, not each other
- "We are good roommates."

### During
- Practice together (breath, polarity, presence)
- Safe vulnerability re-emerges
- Sexual connection deepens not from technique but from presence
- Communication becomes embodied, not only verbal

### After
- Partnership feels like a living thing, not an arrangement
- Intimacy is a daily practice, not a weekend event
- Each partner carries their own practice and meets in shared one
- The relationship itself becomes a spiritual path

## A3 — Seekers of Aliveness

### Before
- Autopilot life
- Intellectually rich, experientially starved
- Skeptical of spirituality, hungry for depth
- Has read everything, embodied nothing

### During
- Encounters a practice that doesn't require belief
- Science + tradition makes the door enterable
- Daily practice becomes non-negotiable
- Begins to feel, not just think

### After
- Life feels textured — ordinary moments carry weight
- Work re-aligns to meaning over optimization
- Relationships deepen — fewer, truer
- "I am no longer waiting for my life to start."
