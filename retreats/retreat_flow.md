# Daily Rhythm — Core Days (Phase 2–3)

Time is not tight. The schedule holds space; it does not command it.

## 6:00 — Dawn practice (optional)
Victoria leads a 45-minute opening. Silent arrival. No words.

## 7:30 — Tea & silence
Communal tea. Silent until 10am.

## 8:30 — Simple breakfast
Plant-based, handmade, slow.

## 10:00 — Morning teaching & practice
2-hour container. Teaching (30 min max), practice (90+ min).

## 12:30 — Lunch
Longest meal of the day. Ceremonial.

## 14:00 — Still hour
Unscheduled. Rest, write, walk, be alone.

## 16:00 — Afternoon practice
Somatic work, breathwork, partner work, or integration writing.
Varies by day.

## 18:30 — Dinner
Lighter. Candlelit.

## 20:00 — Evening practice
Circle, darkness, or closing ritual. Most intimate time.

## 22:00 — Silence returns
Quiet hours. No lights after 22:30 in common areas.

---

# Rest Days
One day per 7-day retreat is unscheduled. Optional body treatment,
optional 1:1 with Victoria, rest. Critical for nervous system integration.

# Adaptation Rules
- If the group's nervous system is tight — slow down, do less.
- If the group is asleep — do not push. Rest is the teaching.
- If weather is perfect — move a practice outside.
- If weather is rough — let it be part of the container.

Victoria's read of the room overrides the schedule. Always.
