# Pre-Arrival Sequence

Begins the moment a seat is confirmed. The retreat begins before the guest arrives.

## Sequence Map

### T-45 days — Welcome Letter
**Delivery:** email + physical mail
**Content:** Brief. Poetic. Names the retreat's intention.
Enclosure: one ritual item (small stone / candle).

### T-30 days — Preparation Practice
**Delivery:** email
**Content:** Audio — 10-minute daily practice to begin now.
Invitation to write one page: "What am I ready to leave behind?"

### T-21 days — Practical Details
**Delivery:** email
**Content:** Arrival instructions, what to pack, dietary confirmation,
emergency contacts, local info.
_Practical email — kept separate from emotional ones._

### T-14 days — Intention Letter
**Delivery:** email
**Content:** An invitation to write Victoria one paragraph:
"What am I hoping to meet here?"
Responses go to a private address, read personally by Victoria before arrival.

### T-7 days — The Arriving
**Delivery:** email
**Content:** Short, sensory. "The villa is being prepared. The practices are
being held for you. We are waiting with reverence."

### T-2 days — Travel Blessing
**Delivery:** email
**Content:** Single paragraph. "May your travel be a prayer."

### T-0 — Arrival
Victoria personally greets. No script needed.

# What we ask of guests

- No phones after arrival dinner (optional)
- Simple clothing — we provide practice-wear if needed
- One journal, one pen, nothing else needed

# What we send home with them
- Printed photograph of the group
- Handwritten note from Victoria (written during the retreat)
- Integration plan sheet (completed together on Day 6)
- Next-touchpoint date on their calendar
