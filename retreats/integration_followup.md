# Integration Arc

The retreat continues for a year after departure. This is how we hold it.

## Day +3 — Arrival Home
**Channel:** Email
**Content:** "You are home. Be gentle. This week is for integration."
Simple practical tips: sleep, eat well, don't make big decisions.

## Day +14 — Postcard
**Channel:** Physical mail
**Content:** Handwritten postcard from Victoria (actually handwritten during retreat).

## Day +21 — Practice Reminder
**Channel:** Email
**Content:** Audio — 15-min integration practice.
"This is for the moments the retreat begins to feel far away."

## Day +30 — 1:1 Integration Call
**Channel:** 30-min video call with Victoria or senior facilitator
**Content:** Open space. What unfolded? What is surprising? What is hard?

## Day +60 — Group Reunion Circle
**Channel:** 90-min group video call
**Content:** The cohort re-gathers. Sharing, light practice, held space.

## Day +90 — Integration Complete
**Channel:** Email
**Content:** "The retreat is now inside you."
Invitation to next step if resonant: membership, next retreat, 1:1.

## Day +180 — Light touch
**Channel:** Email
**Content:** Brief check-in. No offer. Simply presence.

## Day +365 — Year Mark
**Channel:** Email or handwritten card (for inner-circle alumni)
**Content:** A reflection on what a year carries. Invitation to return if ready.

---

# Integration Ethics
- Never sell in the first 30 days post-retreat. The vulnerability is too fresh.
- Offer the membership at day 60 if the community needs it.
- Next retreat invitation: only if they've been offered at least two non-sales touches first.
- If someone struggles — a therapist referral list is offered gently, not apologetically.
- Victoria reads every integration-call note personally.
