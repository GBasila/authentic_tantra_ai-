# Retreat Page Template

## Metadata
- **Retreat name:** [poetic phrase — e.g., «Тишина возвращения»]
- **Dates:** [YYYY-MM-DD to YYYY-MM-DD]
- **Location:** [village / region, country]
- **Cohort size:** [N seats]
- **Price:** [€]
- **Application deadline:** [date]

---

## Page Structure

### 1. Opening (hero)
_[A single sentence. Sensory. Not informational.]_
_[Background: hero image from location or a body-moment.]_

### 2. The invitation (1 paragraph)
_[Recognition: "Возможно, вы здесь, потому что..."]_
_[Name the longing in their exact language.]_

### 3. What this retreat is
_[3-5 short paragraphs. Poetic but clear.]_
_[What they will encounter: practices, silence, community, transformation.]_

### 4. What this retreat is not
_[2-3 sentences. Positioning. No neo-tantra, no spiritual bypass, no performance.]_

### 5. The arc (5 phases)
_[Visual: timeline showing Arrival → Descent → Core → Integration → Closing]_
_[Short poetic description per phase, not a schedule]_

### 6. The place
_[Location description. Sensory: light, scent, sound, land.]_
_[Images.]_

### 7. What is included
_[Accommodation, meals, practices, 1:1 time, follow-up.]_
_[Simple bullets. No padded value stack.]_

### 8. The investment
_[Price, plain numbers.]_
_[Payment plan if offered.]_
_[Scholarship tier mentioned (10% of seats).]_

### 9. Who this is for (and not)
_[2-3 paragraphs matching to avatar.]_
_[Honest "this may not be for you if..." statement.]_

### 10. Victoria
_[Short. One paragraph. Not a CV.]_

### 11. The application
_[Application-based only for premium retreats.]_
_[Link to form. Response within 72h.]_

### 12. Final invitation
_[The closing. One sentence. Read like a blessing.]_
_[Example: "Если ваше тело услышит 'да' — напишите нам. Мы ждём с реверансом."]_

---

## Visual direction
- Single hero image, full-bleed
- Serif headlines, whitespace-heavy
- Brand palette throughout
- No testimonial sliders
- No "only X seats left" widgets
- Optional: one hand-picked quote mid-page, from a previous retreat participant (with consent)
