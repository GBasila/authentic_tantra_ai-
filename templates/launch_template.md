# Launch Template

Use this file as the starting point for every flagship launch.
Copy into `/sales/launches/YYYY-QN-offer/sequence.md` and customize.

## Launch Metadata
- **Offer:** [name]
- **Price:** [€]
- **Dates:** waitlist open [X] · cart open [Y] · cart close [Z]
- **Cohort size:** [target]
- **Revenue target:** [€]

## 8-Week Cycle Checklist

### T-8 to T-6 · Seeding
- [ ] No launch mention
- [ ] Content: pillar-heavy
- [ ] Audience listening ramped up
- [ ] Internal team briefing

### T-5 to T-4 · Warm-up
- [ ] Concept planted in content ("there is a practice...")
- [ ] No dates yet
- [ ] Email weekly letter rhythm holds

### T-3 · Waitlist Opens
- [ ] Letter from Victoria — "the doors are opening"
- [ ] Waitlist form live
- [ ] IG post soft-announcing
- [ ] Target: 500-1000 waitlist

### T-2 · Free Training Series
- [ ] Video 1 (Monday)
- [ ] Video 2 (Wednesday)
- [ ] Video 3 (Friday)
- [ ] Light daily emails pointing to each

### T-1 · Anticipation
- [ ] Daily short letters
- [ ] Founder story cluster
- [ ] Student story (with consent)

### T-0 · Cart Open Week
- [ ] Day 1: Opening letter
- [ ] Day 2: Teaching letter
- [ ] Day 3: Q&A letter
- [ ] Day 4: Silence day
- [ ] Day 5: Story letter
- [ ] Day 6: Gentle reminder
- [ ] Day 7: Close letter

### T+1 · Integration
- [ ] No new offers
- [ ] Welcome cohort
- [ ] Deliver first week with excellence

### T+2 to T+4 · Retrospective
- [ ] Write retrospective.md
- [ ] Revenue review
- [ ] Voice audit of all launch copy (post-mortem)
- [ ] Team reflection session

---

## Forbidden for every launch
- No fake scarcity
- No bonus stacks
- No urgency timers
- No "extended by popular demand"
- Never publish a launch email without Brand Guardian audit
